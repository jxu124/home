About me

我是西安交通大学人工智能学院博士研究生，研究方向为机器人和 人机交互，师从 兰旭光教授 。近期专注于VLA机器人操作模型与机器人多模态交互模型研究。

I'm a soon-to-be PhD graduate from the College of Artificial Intelligence, Xi'an Jiaotong University. My research focuses on robotics and human-robot interaction, supervised by Prof. Xuguang Lan . My recent research interests lie in VLA robotic manipulation models and multimodal interaction models.
